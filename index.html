You Are Super Duper!

We are on a journey to create a memorable day of building and launching rockets, in a shared effort to lift spirits and push limits for all children, particularly those battling with cancer.
The day is focused on unity through various team building activities designed to spark the imagination and conquer a common goal, and most of all HAVE FUN!


Come join us to lift up and lift off!

On November 21st, Super Duper is hosting an event for children battling cancer to be team captain to build and launch rockets! Each captain has the opportunity to be bring a team of one to two super duper sidekick friends who together will design, construct and launch kid-friendly rockets. Sidekicks can be anyone -- a friend, supporter, cousin, sister, brother, etc -- as long as they are between the ages of 5 and 10 years old.

The event will be hosted at NASAâ€™s Moffett Field. Rockets, rocket instructors and lunch will be provided by the event. We only ask that you bring one to two chaperones per child.

Applications are now open. As there are limited spots, we will be taking teams on a first come, first serve basis so be sure to apply now!
